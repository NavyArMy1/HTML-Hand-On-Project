Pizza is an Italian food that was created in Italy (The Naples area). It is made with different toppings.

SALAD

Fruit salad is a dish consisting of various kinds of fruit, sometimes served in a liquid, either their juices or a syrup.
In different forms, fruit salad can be served as an appetizer or a side salad.
